About Founder

Pawan Kumar Kedia

Pawan Kumar Kedia is the founder of Sri R.K Modern Rice Mill. Born in 1956, he was a graduate back in the days when it was a rare sight. Finishing his graduation from a university in Rajasthan, he soon found his way into the business in the year 1978. He owned a number of businesses including Sugar Mills, Granite Machines, Paper mills, and more besides Rice Mills. Owing to his hard-working professional personality & a humble down-to-earth persona, he was able to scale Sri R.K Modern Rice Mill & help it acquire great heights with time.

Managing Director

Punit Kedia

A post-graduate from the Indian Institute of Planning & Management, Punit Kedia believes in adapting to technology with time to scale the business. He brings in the new-age mindset to the family business, helping it adapt better in the digital age. He has adept analytical skills & management ability, which he uses to help Sri R.K Modern Rice Mill grow with time. He aims at seeking newer technologies & implementing them to help them make working more efficient & profitable.

About KediaRice

Sri R K Modern Rice Mill in Khanapur, Nizamabad is one of the prominent players in the Rice Mill refining industry in Telangana, India.The business came into existence in 1998 and has, since then, been a known name in its field.

We are an agile player which aims to swiftly create its presence in the Global Grains market by providing quality grades of refined rice produced through its in-house state-of-the-art technology & professional expertise.

We are known to satisfactorily cater to the demands of our customer base. The business strives to make for a positive experience through its offerings.

Customer centricity is at the core of Sri R K Modern Rice Mill which has led the business to build long-term relationships. Ensuring a positive customer experience, making available goods and/or services that are of top-notch quality is given prime importance.
